05 — Initiative

The Inspire
Initiative

Career Platform · Singapore · APAC Expansion
Closing the gap between
students and opportunity.

Most students don't lack ambition — they lack access. Career guidance is fragmented across career fairs, LinkedIn, professors, and hearsay. Inspire is built to fix that. It's a centralised student career development platform that brings together curated content, structured mentorship, and matched opportunities in one place.

The platform is built on a simple insight: students need to first understand a field before they can navigate it, then build the skills and relationships to compete in it, and finally act on real opportunities with confidence. That logic drives everything — from how content is surfaced to how mentors are matched to how opportunities are recommended. Currently live in Singapore, with APAC expansion underway.
